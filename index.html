About Our Ministry

Founded on faith, built on love, and dedicated to serving God's people.

Our Story

Hope for Life Jesus Ministry International was established with a divine calling to spread the Gospel of Jesus Christ and transform lives through His love. Located in the heart of Nairobi, Kenya, our ministry serves as a beacon of hope for individuals seeking spiritual growth and biblical education.

Our Mission

To equip believers with sound biblical knowledge, foster spiritual growth, and create a community where faith flourishes and lives are transformed through the power of Jesus Christ.
